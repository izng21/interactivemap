A Message from Leadership

Director's Welcome

Dr. Agnes Y. Gonzaga
Campus Director
Polytechnic University
of the Philippines
Maragondon Campus
"

On behalf of the faculty, staff, and the entire PUP Maragondon community, I extend my warmest welcome to each and every one of you.

This campus stands as a testament to our commitment to providing quality, accessible, and relevant education to every Filipino. We believe that knowledge is the most powerful tool for transforming lives and communities, and it is our mission to equip you with the skills, values, and character needed to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

As you explore our facilities, programs, and opportunities, know that you are joining a community dedicated to excellence, integrity, and service. Your journey here is not just about academic achievement — it is about growing as a person, building lifelong connections, and contributing meaningfully to society.

We are honored to walk alongside you in this journey. Welcome to PUP Maragondon — your home of learning and growth.

Dr. Agnes Y. Gonzaga, Campus Director
